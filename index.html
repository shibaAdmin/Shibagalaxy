ShibaGalaxy Story

ShibaGalaxy is building a metaverse for all shibas on Binance Smart Chain. ShibaGalaxy is united by a history long forgotten , ShibaGalaxy is currently under attack and all hope lies on the shiba warriors to keep angry bears and dragons from invading the metaverse.


The Shiba-Bull is calling on all Shibas to join the metaverse and face enemy invaders together.

Shiba Warriors

Shiba Warriors keep the angry bears out of the Shibaverse, hence protecting the Shiba Galaxy economy. Users get a random Shiba warrior anytime they buy a Shiba Chest.

Shiba Bull

Shiba Bull is the oldest shiba and the protector of the Shiba Vault.

Shiba Magic-Stone

The magic stone is essential for The Autoplay mode. Users with the magic stone will have their Shiba warriors battle Angry bears while they are away, earning users SHIBGX and CAKE.

Features

  • Easy Play-To-Earn NFT game

    ShibaGalaxy is an Easy to play NFT Game. All you need is the Shiba Chest to get started. Users will be able to defend the ShibaGalaxy metaverse by fighting against the angry bears to be rewarded in SHIBGX.

  • Shiba-Bull Buy Back

    Shiba bull is the oldest Shiba and the protector of the Shiba Vault. The Shiba Vault keeps the ShibaGalaxy economy afloat Only Shiba-Bull has the access to the vault, and it executes the Buy back and Burn mechanism to keep price healthy.

  • NFT marketplace

    ShibaGalaxy NFTs Marketplace is where you create, sell, and buy NFTs like shiba warriors and other in game assets. Moreover, you can launch NFT auctions, earn commissions from affiliate programs, and build your favorite NFT collection.



  • Fight Angry Bears and Dragon Lords

    Use Shibas to battle angry bears and get rewarded in SHIBGX

  • ShibaGalaxy Autoplay

    Users with the magic stones will be able to have their shiba warriors fight on auto mode. Earning both in native token SHIBGX and CAKE.

Metaverse

  • Armory

    A place in the ShibaGalaxy metaverse where battle equipment is stored, Shiba warriors can Upgrade to better weapons in the Armory.

  • Training Ground

    Training ground for shiba warriors to get a simulated Battlefield experience. After each training Shibas will increase their rank and earning ability.

  • Apparel store

    Apparel stores can be owned by players and they make profit from every apparel sold in their store.
